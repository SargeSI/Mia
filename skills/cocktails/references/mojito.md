# Mojito

## Classic (Cuban)
- 50 ml white rum (Havana Club 3 Años)
- Half lime, cut into wedges
- 8-10 mint leaves + sprig for garnish
- 15 ml sugar syrup (or 2 tsp sugar)
- Soda water (~150 ml)
- Crushed ice

## Sprite variant («ленивое мохито»)
Sprite replaces soda water + sugar entirely. No additional sweetener needed.

**Proportions** (per glass, highball ~350 ml):
- 50 ml white rum
- Half lime, 3-4 wedges
- 8-10 mint leaves + garnish sprig
- Sprite (~150 ml, top up after ice)
- Crushed ice

**Technique:**
1. Chill glass (optional — nice but not critical)
2. **Slap mint** between palms — releases oil without bitterness. Don't shred with knife
3. Lime wedges + mint in glass, **muddle lightly** — press lime, not mint. Mint only needs pressing, not grinding
4. Ice to the top — crushed preferred, smaller is better
5. Pour rum (50 ml)
6. Top with Sprite
7. Stir once, bottom-to-top — don't shake
8. Garnish with mint sprig + lime wheel

**Pitfalls:**
- Don't muddle mint aggressively → bitterness
- Don't shake — it's a built drink, not a shaken cocktail
- Drink immediately — ice melts, Sprite goes flat
