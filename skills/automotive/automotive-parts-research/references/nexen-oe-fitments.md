## Key Takeaway

When researching Nexen tires for wet-weather use: N'Fera Sport is the one with Porsche/VW OE validation. The OE qualification process for Porsche includes stringent wet grip and aquaplaning tests — this explains the tire's #1 aquaplaning ranking in AutoBild sportscars 2025. SU1 and Primus models do NOT have this level of OE validation.
