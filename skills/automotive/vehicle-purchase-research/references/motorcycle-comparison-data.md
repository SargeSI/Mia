# Touring Motorcycle Comparison Data (June 2026)

Structured comparison of 7 heavy touring motorcycles — candidates discussed with Sergey as potential ST1300 replacement. All prices are Russian market, June 2026.

---

## 1. Honda GL1800A Gold Wing (2012-2017 restyle)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Engine | Flat-6, 1832 cm³, SOHC 12v, liquid-cooled |
| Power / Torque | ~118 hp / ~167 Nm |
| Curb weight | ~420 kg |
| Transmission | 6-speed, shaft drive |
| Tank | 25 L |
| Passenger | ★★★★★ (best in class) |

**Known issues:**
- **Transmission:** weak factory plate on copy shaft → incomplete gear engagement → expensive repair. Known weakness of all GL1800 family.
- **Wobble:** at speed — cured by steering head bearing replacement and correct torque.
- **Service access:** engine buried deep — disassemble half the motorcycle for major work.

**Russian market:** 1.2-1.8M ₽ for 2012-2017 restyle examples.

**Verdict:** Ultimate passenger comfort but 420 kg is a physical challenge. Maintenance complexity of a small car.

## 4. Kawasaki GTR1400 (Concours 14)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Engine | Inline-4, 1352 cm³, VVT (Mitsubishi) |
| Power / Torque | 155-160 hp / 136 Nm |
| Curb weight | 305-313 kg |
| Transmission | 6-speed, shaft drive (Tetra-Lever) |
| Tank | 22 L |
| 0-100 km/h | 2.91 sec |
| Passenger | ★★★★ |

**Known issues:**
- **KIPASS:** wireless key system. Microswitch under ignition ring sticks → won't start. Clean with contact cleaner. Key fob battery must be fresh. Pre-2010 models more affected.
- **Rear brake lock-up:** early models (2008-2009) had debris trap in master cylinder mechanism. Recall issued — verify it was done.
- **Warped rotors:** early models under hard use.
- Discontinued in Europe after 2016 (Euro-4), continued in NA through 2022.

**Unique:** Tetra-Lever rear suspension — shaft drive jacking effect neutralized, no rear squat/lift during acceleration/braking. Aerodynamically clean panniers — stable at 200+ km/h.

**Russian market:** 500-900K ₽. Cheapest in the class.

**Verdict:** Rational budget choice. Direct ST1300 ideology successor: Japanese I4, shaft, built to eat kilometers. But models are 10-16 years old now.
